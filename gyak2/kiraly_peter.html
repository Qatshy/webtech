2012 Formula 1 World Championship

The 2012 Formula One world Championship was the 63rd FIA Formula One World Championship, which started on March 18 and ended on November 25. It is consedered by many as one of the best F1 seasons of all time. The season broke many record, such as:

  • Most World Champions competing in a season (6) source
  • Most different race winners in consecutive races(starting from the first race of the season)(7)source
